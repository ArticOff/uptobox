How to get my token?
============

To get your token, you need to follow these steps

1. Make sure you're logged on to the `Uptobox website <https://uptobox.com/>`_.
2. When you are on the Uptobox website

    .. image:: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ArticOff/uptobox/main/docs/images/token-step1.png
      :width: 1000

3. Click on "my account"

    .. image:: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ArticOff/uptobox/main/docs/images/token-step2.png
      :width: 500

4. You will end up on this page

    .. image:: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ArticOff/uptobox/main/docs/images/token-step3.png
      :width: 1000

5. Click on the copy icon to the right of "Token".

    .. image:: https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ArticOff/uptobox/main/docs/images/token-step4.png
      :width: 500
  
And that's it. You now have your token !
